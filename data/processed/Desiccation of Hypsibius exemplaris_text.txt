and Evolution, Volume 16, Issue 1, January 2024, https://doi.org/10.1093/gbe/evad217
In the originally published online version of this manuscript, details in the caption to Figure 7 were errored.
These should read: “[…]CAHS is represented in red, MAHS in purple, SAHS in green, EtAHS alpha in yellow, EtAHS beta in 
pink, and MRE11 in blue.[…]” instead of: “[…] CAHS is represented in blue, MAHS in purple, SAHS in green, EtAHS alpha in 
red, EtAHS beta in orange, and MRE11 in black.[…]”.
These errors are outlined only in this correction notice to preserve the version of record.
