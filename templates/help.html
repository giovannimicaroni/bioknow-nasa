What is BioKnowdes?
BioKnowdes is an innovative platform that helps researchers discover connections between biological experiments, keywords, and scientific concepts through interactive visualizations and AI-powered insights.
How do I start exploring data?
Click on the Search button on the homepage or navigate to the Graph section from the menu. You can search for specific keywords, experiments, or concepts to visualize their connections.
Do I need an account?
No account is needed for basic exploration. You can browse, search, and interact with all visualizations without signing up.

üîç Using the Graph

How do I navigate the graph?
You can interact with the graph by:
  • Clicking on nodes to view details
  • Dragging to move nodes around
  • Zooming with mouse wheel
  • Panning by dragging the background
Can I filter results?
Yes! Use the search bar and filters on the Graph page to narrow down results by keywords, date ranges, or specific categories.

üìä Heatmap

What is the Heatmap?
The Heatmap visualizes the frequency and intensity of connections between different biological concepts, helping you identify patterns and hotspots in the research data.
How to interpret colors?
Darker/brighter colors indicate stronger connections or higher frequency. Use the legend on the page to understand the scale specific to your search.
Can I export the heatmap?
Look for the export or download button on the Heatmap page to save visualizations for your research or presentations.

ü§ñ AskLumi AI

What is Lumi?
Lumi is your AI-powered research assistant that can answer questions about the data, help you discover connections, and provide insights about biological experiments.
How do I chat with Lumi?
Click on the chat balloon in the bottom right corner of any page, or visit the AskLumi page from the menu. Type your question and press Enter.
What can I ask Lumi?
You can ask about specific experiments, connections between concepts, scientific terms, platform features, and recommendations for related research.
